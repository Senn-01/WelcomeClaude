Welcome to Claude!

Claude is an AI assistant created by Anthropic, designed to be helpful, honest, and harmless. With advanced language understanding and generation capabilities, Claude can help with a wide range of tasks from writing and analysis to problem-solving and creative exploration.

Fun Facts

Did you know? Claude is built with a strong commitment to being ethical, truthful, and beneficial to humanity. The name Claude is inspired by the idea of "cloud" - representing the collective knowledge and capabilities of AI.
